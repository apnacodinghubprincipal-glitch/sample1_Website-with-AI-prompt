A modern, multipurpose Girls' Higher Secondary School.

Agrasain Balika Shiksha Sadan

Established in 1994, Agrasain Balika Shiksha Sadan (ABSS) is an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, New Delhi (ICSE & ISC).

"The purpose of education is to replace an empty mind with an open one." Our institution aims to develop young minds into powerful and graceful women who contribute to society as upstanding and righteous citizens.
